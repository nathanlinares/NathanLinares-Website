Starting in September of 2018, I began attending the University of California, Riverside pursuing a bachelors degree in United States History. Still enrolled, I am expected to graduate in 2022, then hoping to continue onto my masters degree and teaching credential. After accomplishing this, I plan to teach high school, spectifically Advanced Placement United States History. I believe every child is entitled to quality education, and they should be given every opportunity possible to realize their potential and succeed. Too many teenagers and young adults are not made aware of the proper resources available to them, so my plan is to make students aware of these sources early on. My AP US History teacher at Redlands High School left such a lasting impact on me with his effective and honest teaching paired with pure passion for seeing his students succeed. If I can help to make a positive impact on any one person's life, then my hard work will have had paid off.

To help pay for expenses through college, I work part time at Office Depot OfficeMax in my home town of Redlands, California. My focus is varies, but I mainly work on the logistics of the store, and help with technology support when help is needed. I am responsible for unloading new truck shipments my manager receives, stocking merchandise, and ensuring that guests can find the items they need. When I help out with tech support, I check computers in to be looked at by our tech supervisors, process data transfers, and do anything that I can to meet our guests' needs and get their devices back up and running so they can continue their valuable work. Office Depot values customer experience, so I always do everything in my power to make sure customers are approached in a friendly manner and made aware that our knowledgable staff is here to help in any capacity. Although this is a job just for while I am in college, I take pride in our team at the Redlands location, and enjoy the people I work with any given shift. Being able to help others brings joy and peace to my heart, so focusing not only my job, but my future career path around make a person's day easier and brighter is only natural to me.
